Krabby Patty

Krabby Patties are a staple in Bikini Bottom. The secret formula has long been classified, until now! Use this recipe to make the world famous sandwich for yourself.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground crab meat
  • 2 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
  • 1 tsp. Black pepper
  • 1 tsp. Sea Salt
  • 2 Slices of preferred cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • 4 Sesame seed buns

Instructions

  1. Combine the crab meat with the seasonings and form into four patties.
  2. Place patties on grill and cook for 5 minutes on each side, adding cheese for the last minute.
  3. Remove patties from grill and blace on sesame seed buns.
  4. Add the rest of the toppings as desired.
  5. Enjoy!
