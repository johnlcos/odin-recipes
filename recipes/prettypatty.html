Pretty Patties

Pretty Patties are a innovative take on the world famous Krabby Patty! These delicious burgers come in a variety of eye popping colors. There is a color for everyone!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground crab meat
  • 2 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
  • 1 tsp. Black pepper
  • 1 tsp. Sea Salt
  • 2 Slices of preferred cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • 4 Sesame seed buns
  • Paint of preferred color

Instructions

  1. Combine the crab meat with the seasonings and form into four patties.
  2. Place patties on grill and cook for 5 minutes on each side, adding cheese for the last minute.
  3. Remove patties from grill and blace on sesame seed buns.
  4. Add the rest of the toppings as desired.
  5. Once the burger is assembled, hit it with a thick coat of paint of the desired color. Wow! So pretty!
  6. Enjoy!
