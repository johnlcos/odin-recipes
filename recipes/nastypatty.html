Nasty Patty

The Nasty Patty is a new, more premium take on the classic Krabby Patty. This recipe involves preparing a standard Krabby Patty and then "enhancing" it to take the flavors up a notch!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground crab meat
  • 2 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
  • 1 tsp. Black pepper
  • 1 tsp. Sea Salt
  • 2 Slices of preferred cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • 4 Sesame seed buns
  • Volcano Sauce
  • Seahorse Radish
  • Toenail Clippings

Instructions

  1. Combine the crab meat with the seasonings and form into four patties.
  2. Place patties on grill and cook for 5 minutes on each side, adding cheese for the last minute.
  3. Remove patties from grill and blace on sesame seed buns.
  4. Add the rest of the toppings as desired.
  5. Add the volcano sauce, seahorse radish and toenail clippings.
  6. Drop the burger in the toilet.
  7. Retrieve the burger from the toilet and dry it off with used gym socks.
  8. Bone apple tea!
